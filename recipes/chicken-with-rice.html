Chicken With Rice

Homepage

As for what it concerns me, this is the best food I have ever eaten in my life, it tastes like heaven and it is really easy to do so, why not explain it?

Ingredients needed (not all of them are):

  • Rice (any of them work)
    • Normal Rice
    • Brown Rice
    • Arborio Rice
    • Japanese Rice
  • Chicken
  • Oil (at your disposal)
  • Water
  • Seasoning
    • Curcuma longa
    • Salt

It may seem like there are a lot of ingredients but do not let them fool you, it is as easy as it can get to cook them.

Now, let's keep on going with how it's done:

  1. Let's start with the rice
    1. put some oil inside the pot
    2. Put as much glasses of rice as you want
    3. Put the rice in the pot
    4. Put water in the pot (double the amount of water glasses than rice glasses)
    5. Put the pot on the stove
    6. Add some salt
    7. Wait until the rice is cooked
      1. 7 minutes high fire (above 95ºC, boiling water)
      2. 15 minutes low fire (below 50ºC)
    8. Once is cooked add some Curcuma longa and stir it

  2. When the rice is at low fire, we will start cooking the chicken
    1. First cut the chicken into stripes
    2. Put some oil in the pan
    3. Put the chicken stripes in the pan
    4. Add a little salt to the chicken
    5. Stir it for the chicken to get cooked
    6. Once you removed the chicken you can add a little more Curcuma if you want
  3. Mix the chicken and the rice on a plate
  4. Stir it a little

As you can see it is not a difficult dish to cook and believe me when I say, it is the most delicious dish ever.
